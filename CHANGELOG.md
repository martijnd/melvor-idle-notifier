# Changelog

All notable changes to Idle Notifier will be documented in this file.

The format is based on [Keep a Changelog](https://keepachangelog.com/en/1.1.0/).

## [1.0.0]

### Added

Get notified when you die or when combat stops (e.g. area cleared)
Get notified when a skill stops—because the resource ran out, your inventory is full, or you stopped manually
A separate message and sound when you die
Pop-up notifications even when the game tab is in the background (enable in mod settings)
Hear a sound when something goes idle
See notifications in the game so you’re always aware of what’s happening
Option to only get notified when you’re not focused on the game (e.g. in another tab or app)
Your choices are saved and restored next time you play
